Common questions

Everything you need to know before booking a call — answered directly.

How much does AI automation cost for a small business?▾
AI automation projects with Sitfox typically range from $3,000 to $25,000 depending on complexity. Most small businesses see full ROI within 60–90 days through recovered labour costs and new revenue captured by AI-handled leads. We always provide a projected ROI before any engagement begins.
Will AI replace my existing staff?▾
No. Sitfox AI systems handle repetitive, time-consuming tasks — not skilled people. The goal is to free your team from admin work so they focus on higher-value activities. Most clients report increased team satisfaction after implementation.
What is an AI voice agent and how does it work?▾
A custom AI voice agent answers inbound phone calls using natural, conversational AI. It qualifies leads, books appointments, captures contact details, and routes urgent calls to a human — all without staff involvement. Sitfox configures each agent specifically for your business scripts, FAQs, and workflows.
How long does it take to implement an AI system?▾
The Sitfox 21-Day AI-Stack method delivers most systems live within 2–4 weeks. Simple automations can go live in 5–7 days. Complex multi-system integrations may take 4–6 weeks. Every project begins with a scoped timeline so you know exactly what to expect.
Do I need any technical knowledge?▾
None at all. Sitfox is a done-for-you AI implementation partner. We handle all technical work — from tool selection to building, testing, and deploying. You describe your business challenges; we handle everything else.
What industries does Sitfox specialize in?▾
Sitfox currently serves Medical Clinics, Manufacturing, Home Services, Real Estate, and Law & Accounting. Each vertical has a dedicated implementation framework developed from real deployments — not generic templates.
What happens after my AI system goes live?▾
Every Sitfox deployment includes a 30-day post-launch support period at no additional cost. We monitor performance, fix issues, and optimize. After 30 days, clients can opt into ongoing retainer support — your systems are fully yours.
