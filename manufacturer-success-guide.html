Part 01

Lead Generation & Sales

Stop losing leads you never even knew you had.

Idea 01 of 17
24/7 AI Sales Assistant on Your Website
A buyer visits at 9pm, can't find an answer, and leaves. 78% of B2B buyers choose the first supplier that responds — not the cheapest, not the best product. The fastest. The average B2B response time is 42 hours.
How it works
An AI assistant lives on your website, trained on your exact products, pricing, and certifications. It answers questions instantly at any hour. When a buyer is ready, it books a call directly into your calendar.
✓
Lead capture increases 35–55% vs. a static contact form
✓
5-min response vs. 30-min = 100× more likely to convert
✓
Sales team spends 40–60% more time actually selling
Idea 02 of 17
WhatsApp Automation for Business Inquiries
WhatsApp has a 98% open rate. Email has ~20%. Most manufacturers handle it through personal phones with no system — messages sit unanswered for hours while competitors reply in minutes.
How it works
Your WhatsApp Business number connects to an automation system. A buyer messages you and instantly receives a menu: view catalog, request a quote, check an order, or speak to your team. Every path handled automatically. All conversations logged.
✓
Catalog delivery: hours → under 10 seconds
✓

✓

Part 02

Client Communication & Service

Clients who can't get easy answers switch suppliers.

Idea 05 of 17
Automated Order Status Updates
Order status is the #1 reason clients call. Each call takes 3–5 minutes. For a manufacturer with 50 active orders, that's potentially 4 hours a week just telling clients where their orders are.
How it works
Your order data connects to the system. When a client sends their order number via WhatsApp or your website, they receive an instant, accurate response — packed, dispatched, expected delivery — in seconds.
✓
Status calls drop 60–80%
✓
Available 24/7, across all time zones
✓
Instant, accurate information builds client confidence

The Follow-Up Gap
Idea 06 of 17
CRM-Based Follow-Up Sequences
80% of B2B sales require 5+ follow-up attempts. The average sales team makes 1.3 attempts. That gap is enormous revenue that evaporates simply because nobody followed up again.
How it works
When a lead enters your system, a pre-built sequence begins automatically. Inquiry → product info → check-in. Quote sent → 2-day follow-up. Any reply pauses the sequence and notifies your team.
✓
Lead conversion up 25–45% — no extra marketing spend
✓
Zero leads forgotten, no matter how busy your team is
